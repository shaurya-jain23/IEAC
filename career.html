Why Work With Us?

  • Practical Application: Engage in real-world energy assessments to evaluate usage and identify efficiency improvements, gaining valuable hands-on experience.
  • State-of-the-Art Tools: Utilize advanced assessment tools and technologies for precise evaluations, ensuring high-quality results.
  • Collaboration with Experts: Work alongside faculty from IIT and professionals from various companies and government agencies.
  • Networking Opportunities: Build connections with diverse professionals in the green energy sector, opening doors to exciting career opportunities.
  • Serene Work Environment: Enjoy a peaceful setting that fosters creativity, focus, and productivity.
  • Intellectual Interaction: Collaborate with some of the brightest minds in the country, enhancing your learning and professional growth.
  • Impactful Contribution: Participate in meaningful projects that promote energy conservation, environmental sustainability, and industrial efficiency.
  • Skill Development: Sharpen your analytical and problem-solving skills through real-world challenges that support your professional development.
  • Specialized Knowledge: Gain insights into renewable energy technologies and advanced energy management practices, positioning yourself as a leader in the field.

Positions Available

We are looking for highly qualified, motivated individuals to join our team as full-time Energy Assessors.

Energy Assessor

  • Qualifications: Engineering degrees in Electrical, Mechanical, Instrumentation, Energy, Thermal, and allied areas. Certification of Bureau of Energy Efficiency as energy auditor or energy assessor is desirable.
  • Key Responsibilities: Conduct energy assessments, train engineers, review data, manage client relationships, provide feedback, offer recommendations, and ensure follow-up with clients.

Field Engineer

  • Qualifications: Engineering degrees in Electrical, Mechanical, Instrumentation, Energy, Thermal, and allied areas.
  • Key Responsibilities: Collect and record data during energy assessments, collaborate with Energy Assessors to recommend suitable energy conservation measures (ECMs), and assist in implementing assessment protocols.

Trainee Engineer

  • Qualifications: Engineering degrees in Electrical, Mechanical, Instrumentation, Energy, Thermal, and allied areas.
  • Key Responsibilities: Help in collecting data, maintain instruments, and assist in various engineering tasks as needed.
